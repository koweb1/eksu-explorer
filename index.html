Introduction To Ekiti State University

Ekiti State University is a state-owned tertiary institution located in Ado-Ekiti, the capital of Ekiti State in southwestern Nigeria. Established in 1982, the university has grown to become one of the leading institutions of higher learning in Nigeria.

Requirement needed to be a student of ekiti state university

    UTME requirement

  • Five (5) credit passes in SSC examination GCE O/L
  • At least 200 score in the UTME.

    • Direct Entry (D/E) Requirements

  • Direct Entry candidates must possess a minimum of Upper Credit from Polytechnics for Engineering programmes and Lower Credit for other programmes.
  • EKSU will admit only candidates who made at least five (5) Credit passes in SSCE/NECO/NABTEB
